Tastings

My philosophy is that a cake should taste as good as it looks. At Love Street, I say “boo!” to cakes that taste dry and bland!

I use only the best ingredients to whip up my moist, delicious cakes and light, buttery frostings. I offer tastings for wedding cakes as well as large special occasion cakes.

Before our tasting, you will choose two flavor combinations you would like to try from our specialties. I am always in the kitchen testing out new cake recipes, and will do my best to accommodate any special flavor requests.

  • Golden Vanilla
  • Dark Chocolate
  • Cinnamon Spice
  • Toasted Almond*
  • Red Velvet

  • Buttercream Frosting:

  • Brown Sugar
